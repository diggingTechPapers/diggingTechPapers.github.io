Digging into the Technical Papers (in Japanese)

Talk Description:

A commentary and discussion of SIGGRAPH Asia 2024 Technical Paper by a panel of experts. It will consist of research impression, background and its significance. Only available on-site in Japanese.

Contributors:

  • Hideki Todo, SIGGRAPH Asia 2024 Local Committee, Japan
  • Yuki Igarashi, SIGGRAPH Asia 2024 Local Committee, Japan

Presenters:

  • Kei Iwasaki (Saitama University)
  • Kenshi Takayama (CyberAgent)
  • Makoto Okabe (Shizuoka University)
  • Nobuyuki Umetani (The University of Tokyo)
  • Ryoichi Ando (ZOZO, Inc)
  • Edgar Simo-Serra (Waseda University)
  • Yonghao Yue (Aoyama Gakuin University)
  • Yoshihiro Kanamori (University of Tsukuba)

Event Details:

  • Schedule: 3-6 December, 16:30-17:30 each day [Day1, 2, 3, 4]
  • Location:
    • [Day1, 2] G502 (G Block, Level 5)
    • [Day3, 4] G510 (G Block, Level 5)

Note:

    The event location changes between the first half [Day1, 2] and the second half [Day3, 4]
    This event will be held exclusively on-site. There will be no online streaming.
